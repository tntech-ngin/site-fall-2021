Syllabus

This is a tentative schedule and will change.

Week 1 - August 26, 28, 30 - Introduction, Network terminologies, Architecture vs Protocols

September 2 - Labor day, no class

Week 2 - September 4, 6 - Connecting machines, Reliable Delivery, Ethernet, Access protocols

September 8 - Last date to drop class

Week 3 - September 9, 11, 13 - Packet Switching

September 15 - Project 1 Due

Week 4 - September 18, 20, 23 - Internetworking, Transport Protocol

Week 5 - September 30, October 2, 4 - Transport protocols continued, Forwarding, NAT, IPv6

Week 6 - October 7, 9, 11 - Routing, BGP

October 14 - Fall break, no class

Week 7 - October 16, 18 - BGP continued, Global Internet

October 15 - Project 2 Due

Week 8 - October 21, 23, 25 - TCP Congestion control, Congestion avoidance and QoS

Week 9 - October 28, 30, November 1 - Applications - Web, DNS, Email

Week 10 - November 4, 6, 8 - Multimedia applications

Week 11 - November 11, 13, 15 - Network security

November 15 - Project 3 Due

Week 12 - November 18, 20, 21 - Next generation network - Software Defined Network and Named Data Networking

Week 13 - November 25 - Next generation network - Software Defined Network and Named Data Networking continued.

November 27, 29- Thanksgiving break, no classes

Week 14 - December 2, 4, 6 - Review of meterials, project demo, and wrap-up

Final exam - December 9- 12 (TBD, date and time will change)
